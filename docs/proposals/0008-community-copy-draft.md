## C. Community Guidelines (the full page users accept)

### NaijaXch Community Guidelines

NaijaXch hosts general discussion about NGX-listed companies. It is a place to share **information and opinions** — not to give or receive investment advice. To keep it useful and lawful, every member agrees to the following.

**1. General information only — no advice or solicitation.**
Do not tell others to buy, sell, or hold a security, do not solicit others to trade, and do not present your opinion as professional or personalised advice. Share observations and reasoning, not recommendations. NaijaXch provides general information only and is **not** a broker, dealer, or investment adviser.

**2. No market manipulation.**
Absolutely no "pump-and-dump", coordinated buying/selling, spreading information you know to be false or misleading to move a price, or any other conduct that manipulates or attempts to manipulate the market in any security. This is prohibited and may be **unlawful under Nigerian securities law (ISA / SEC Nigeria rules)**. Suspected manipulation will be removed and may be reported to the authorities.

**3. No spam, ads, or promotion.**
No advertising, referral links, "signal groups", paid tips, fundraising, or repetitive/off-topic posting. Do not promote external trading services or schemes.

**4. No abuse.**
No harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks. Disagree with the idea, not the person.

**5. Protect personal data.**
Do not post anyone's personal or contact information — including your own phone number, email, WhatsApp, BVN, account numbers, or addresses. Do not ask others for theirs.

**6. Be accurate and your own voice.**
Don't post information you know to be false. Don't impersonate others or NaijaXch. Don't post content you don't have the right to share.

**7. Nothing unlawful.**
No content that is illegal under Nigerian law, infringes others' rights, or breaches NGX/SEC rules.

**How we moderate.**
Members can report posts. We may hide or remove posts that break these guidelines and may suspend or remove accounts for repeated or serious breaches, at our discretion. We don't pre-screen every post, and we don't endorse any post. Removing or keeping a post is not a judgment on its accuracy.

**Your content and privacy.**
You keep responsibility for what you post. We show your display name with your posts — never your email. You can delete your own posts, and deleting your account removes or anonymises them. We handle your data under our **[Privacy Policy]** and the Nigeria Data Protection Act. See our **[Terms]** for the full agreement.

**A reminder.**
Markets carry risk. Information and opinions here — including any numbers a user types — are **general information and personal views, not advice**. Always do your own research and consider professional advice for your own situation.
